About MEK

A Lao company building a national distribution network.

MEK Imports-Exports Sole Co., LTD was founded on 4 July 2021 by Mr. Mekakhom Sihalath with a single, clear mission — to make trusted regional and global brands available in every retailer across the Lao PDR, and in doing so build one of the country's most reliable import, export and distribution platforms.

From a single Vientiane head office we now operate three regional branches covering north, central and south Laos, distributing nineteen partner brands across food, beverages, beauty, hair & body, oral care, baby care, household and stationery categories.

"Mek Gong Fa — let MEK be heard across all of Laos." Company Mission

Our growth strategy rests on five pillars — deep distribution, an empowered salesforce, in-store activation, continuous product portfolio diversification, and the steady building of MEK's reputation with retailers and consumers nationwide.

What We Do

End-to-end commercial services for brands entering Laos.

From customs clearance and warehousing to nationwide store coverage and in-market activation, MEK is the single partner brand owners need to launch, grow and protect their share in Lao PDR.

Import & Export

Customs handling, regulatory paperwork, freight forwarding and duty optimisation for brands entering or leaving Lao PDR.

National Distribution

Direct-to-store delivery across 16 of 18 provinces from three regional hubs — Vientiane, Luang Prabang and Pakse.

Logistics & Warehousing

Bonded storage, cold-chain capability and inventory management designed for FMCG turnover and seasonal peaks.

Sales & Activation

Field salesforce, merchandising, sampling and in-store promotions that convert distribution into actual consumer pull.

Brand Partnership

Long-term distributor partnerships with category exclusivity, joint planning and trade marketing investment.

Market Intelligence

Province-by-province data on universe, coverage and sell-through — one of the cleanest retail datasets in Laos.
